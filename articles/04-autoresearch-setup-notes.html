AutoResearch setup notes (starter)

By Chase Arenella•2026-03-12

Notes for setting up an agentic research workflow and turning outputs into publishable, linked artifacts.

Agentic research becomes valuable when it ships outputs: summaries, decision notes, and new articles.

Start small: pick 3 recurring topics, run weekly searches, and publish 1 synthesis post.

Then automate: scheduled runs, templated outputs, and a “publish to site” step.

This Systems Lab is the testing ground: if an automation reliably creates crawlable pages, it compounds authority.
