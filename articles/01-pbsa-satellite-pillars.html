Satellite pillars: the authority layer

By Chase Arenella•2026-03-12

Why a satellite pillar (like this Systems Lab) strengthens the Chase Arenella entity cluster and pushes out weak results over time.

A PBSA hub establishes canonical identity: who you are, what you do, and where the “truth” lives.

A satellite pillar adds authority signals: it goes deep on a theme (systems, automation, media, projects) and links back to the hub.

The win comes from consistency across multiple sites: same name, same context, same cross-links, same structured data.

The generator endgame is simple: deploy the hub, then deploy satellites on a schedule (monthly is ideal) with cross-linking baked in.

This Systems Lab is designed to behave exactly like that future automation: publish content, refresh on cadence, and reinforce the entity graph.
