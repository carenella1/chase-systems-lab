Entity expansion cadence: why monthly wins

By Chase Arenella•2026-03-12

Why the PBSA expansion schedule should usually be 30–45 days per new pillar, plus weekly micro-updates.

Too fast looks artificial; too slow loses compounding.

Monthly expansions give crawl/index time while still signaling growth.

Within each pillar, weekly micro-updates keep the site alive without adding noise.

The cadence that typically works best: 1 new pillar every 30–45 days, and 1 post per week inside each pillar.

This repo also runs an automated monthly refresh to generate a new maintenance page and keep signals active.
