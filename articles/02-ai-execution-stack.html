The AI Execution Stack (v1)

By Chase Arenella•2026-03-12

A practical stack for turning AI into execution power: intake → structure → action → review.

Execution beats ideas. The AI Execution Stack is a loop, not a tool list.

1) Intake: capture raw inputs (notes, transcripts, tasks).

2) Structure: turn inputs into clean categories (decisions, actions, risks, follow-ups).

3) Action: convert structured output into next steps (calendar, tasks, docs).

4) Review: weekly review, update your frameworks, and publish the learnings.

This site exists to document that loop and link it back to the canonical Chase Arenella hub.
